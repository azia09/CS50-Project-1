The Pakistan cricket team is a recognized member of the ICC (international cricket council) since 1952. They are regularly featured in global tournaments such as the ICC World Cup, as well as international tours. The team has made itself distinguishable due to its unpredictable nature, being able to outplay the world's best teams while being equally capable of suffering an embarassing loss to weaker opposition. They are currently coached by retired player Misbah-ul-Haq.
